How to Reach Barala Hospital

🚗
From Jaipur City:

Approx. 35–40 km via NH-52 (Jaipur–Sikar Road). 40–55 minute drive. Follow signs for Chomu on NH-52.

🚌
By Bus:

RSRTC and private buses from Jaipur Sindhi Camp to Chomu. Auto-rickshaw from Chomu Bus Stand (2–3 km).

🏙️
From Sikar:

Approx. 55–65 km south on NH-52 toward Jaipur. The hospital is on the left side (west) of NH-52 entering Chomu.
