Quick Tips

Essential Health Tips

Simple, evidence-based health practices recommended by the Barala Hospital specialist team.

❤️

Heart Health

30 minutes of moderate physical activity daily, a low-salt diet, and regular blood pressure monitoring are among the most effective ways to maintain heart health.

💧

Kidney Care

Adequate hydration (8–10 glasses of water daily) is one of the most important preventive measures against kidney stones — especially important in Rajasthan's warm climate.

🧠

Brain Health

Managing blood pressure, avoiding smoking, and staying physically active are among the most evidence-backed strategies to reduce risk of stroke and cognitive decline.

👶

Child Nutrition

Exclusive breastfeeding for the first six months and timely introduction of nutritious complementary foods are critical foundations for a child's long-term health and development.
