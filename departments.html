Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery

Our Cardiology department is anchored by a dedicated Cardiac Catheterisation Laboratory (Cath Lab) staffed by experienced interventional cardiologists. The department has performed a high volume of PTCA (Percutaneous Transluminal Coronary Angioplasty / coronary stenting) procedures, as reported by the hospital.

  • ✓ Coronary Angiography & Angioplasty (PTCA)
  • ✓ Pacemaker Implantation
  • ✓ Heart Failure Management
  • ✓ Echocardiography & TMT
  • ✓ Hypertension & Cardiac Rehabilitation

Neuro-Surgery & Spine Centre

Headed by Dr. Amit Barala — a Fellowship-trained Endovascular Neurosurgeon and Minimally Invasive Spine Surgeon — our Neurosciences Centre offers precision care for the full spectrum of brain and spinal conditions.

  • ✓ Brain Tumour Surgery
  • ✓ Endovascular Brain Surgery
  • ✓ Minimally Invasive Spine Surgery
  • ✓ Stroke Diagnosis & Management
  • ✓ EEG, EMG & Nerve Conduction Studies

Urology & Nephrology

Led by Dr. Abhishek Barala (MBBS, MS, MCh Urology), the department is fully equipped for minimally invasive urological procedures and renal care, including our 24-hour Dialysis Unit.
