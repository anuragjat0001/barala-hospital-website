Critical Care

Critical Care & Surgical Facilities

Our critical care and surgical infrastructure is designed to handle even the most complex medical situations with precision and speed.

Intensive Care Units (ICU)

Our multi-layered ICU system provides critical care at every age group and condition:

  • ✓
    Adult ICU — Ventilators, multi-parameter monitors, central nurse station, 24-hour specialist support
  • ✓
    NICU — Neonatal ICU with incubators, phototherapy, neonatal monitoring for premature and sick newborns
  • ✓
    PICU — Paediatric ICU for critically ill children requiring intensive monitoring and support
  • ✓
    Obstetric HDU — High-dependency unit for high-risk pregnancies and post-operative obstetric care

Modular Operation Theatres

Four state-of-the-art Operation Theatres designed to international surgical safety standards:

  • ✓
    Modular OTs with Laminar Flow — Positive pressure, HEPA-filtered environments for maximum sterility
  • ✓
    Cardiac Cath Lab — Dedicated catheterisation laboratory for angiography, PTCA, and pacemaker implantation
  • ✓
    Laparoscopic OT — Fully equipped for advanced minimally invasive and robotic-assist surgeries
  • ✓
    Neurosurgical OT — Equipped for complex brain and spine interventions
Diagnostics

Diagnostic Services

Comprehensive in-house diagnostics available 24 hours a day to support rapid, accurate clinical decisions.

CT Scan

In-house CT Scan available 24×7 for emergency and elective cases. Rapid imaging for trauma, neuro, chest, abdominal, and vascular conditions.

Sonography / Ultrasound

Obstetric, abdominal, pelvic, cardiac (Echo), and vascular ultrasound. High-resolution diagnostic imaging by qualified radiologists.

Pathology Laboratory

Full-service 24×7 clinical pathology lab. Haematology, biochemistry, microbiology, immunology, histopathology, and hormone assays — all in-house.

Echocardiography & ECG

Cardiac imaging and electrocardiography available across all departments, supporting rapid assessment of heart conditions and emergencies.

EEG & Nerve Studies

Electroencephalography (EEG), Electromyography (EMG), and Nerve Conduction Studies for neurological diagnosis — available in the Neuro-Surgery department.

Digital X-Ray

Digital radiography with immediate image availability. Used across orthopaedics, chest, emergency, and routine diagnostic workup.

Support Services

Support Facilities

🩸 Blood Bank

Barala Blood Bank operates 24 hours a day, 365 days a year — equipped with high-end blood processing, storage, and cross-matching technology.

We actively encourage community blood donation and hold regular blood donation camps. Our motto: "Raktadaan Mahadaan — Donate Blood, Save Life."

The Blood Bank serves not only Barala Hospital patients but also supports surrounding healthcare facilities and emergency cases across the region.

🚑 ICU on Wheels — Ambulance

Our ambulance fleet is fully equipped critical care transport — not merely a vehicle, but a mobile emergency room that brings hospital-grade care to the patient's location.

  • ✓ Ventilator for respiratory support
  • ✓ Multi-parameter monitors (ECG, SpO₂, NIBP)
  • ✓ Oxygen supply & suction
  • ✓ Defibrillator & resuscitation medications
  • ✓ Trained Emergency Medical Technicians & Nurses
🚨 Call Ambulance: 8875004330

💊 24-Hour Pharmacy

Two in-house pharmacy stores operate round-the-clock, stocking a complete range of prescription medications, surgical consumables, and over-the-counter products. Our pharmacists coordinate closely with treating teams for accurate dispensing and patient medication counselling.

🏥 Dialysis Unit — 24 Hours

Our Dialysis Unit operates round-the-clock and is set up to international standards. It serves both acute kidney injury patients requiring urgent dialysis and chronic kidney disease patients on regular dialysis schedules.

Cashless dialysis available for BSBY and insurance-empanelled patients. Enquire at the reception for scheduling and packages.

Insurance

Insurance & Government Schemes

Barala Hospital is empanelled with an extensive network of insurers and government schemes, making quality healthcare accessible to all.

🏛️

BSBY

Bhamashah Swasthya Bima Yojna — Government of Rajasthan's health insurance scheme. Barala Hospital has been recognised among leading empanelled hospitals under this scheme.

🪖

ECHS

Ex-Servicemen Contributory Health Scheme — empanelled for providing cashless treatment to defence personnel and their families.

📋

50+ TPA Partners

Cashless tie-ups with over 50 Third Party Administrators (TPAs) — covering virtually all major Indian health insurance providers.

For a complete list of empanelled insurers and government schemes, please visit baralahospital.com/empanelment.php or contact our insurance desk at the hospital reception.
