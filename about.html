About Barala Hospital

A legacy of healing — rooted in Chomu, dedicated to every patient across Rajasthan.

Our Story

Healthcare With Heart, Built for Chomu

Barala Hospital & Research Centre was established by the Barala family of physicians who made a principled decision: to return to their hometown of Chomu and build a world-class hospital where quality care was needed most.

Dr. Hanuman Sahai Barala and his family, with over 20 years of combined medical practice, chose not to relocate to large cities — instead investing their expertise and vision into a super multi-speciality hospital that serves not only urban patients but also rural communities across the Jaipur–Sikar belt.

Today, Barala Hospital stands as a 200+ bedded super-speciality centre with 30+ departments, 400+ medical staff, dedicated Cath Lab, Neuro-Surgery, Oncology, Urology, and a 24-hour Blood Bank — serving as the primary advanced healthcare hub for the region.

Our Purpose

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Vision

To be the most trusted super-speciality healthcare institution in rural and semi-urban Rajasthan — delivering world-class medical care with compassion, affordability, and innovation, accessible to every section of society.

Our Mission

To provide advanced multi-speciality healthcare services — curative and preventive — to both urban and rural patients at affordable cost. We combine cutting-edge technology with a deeply human approach to medicine.

Our Values

Patient First. Transparency. Inclusivity. Continuous Innovation. Community Accountability. We believe healthcare is not a privilege — it is a fundamental right of every individual regardless of their background.

Dr. Hanuman Sahai Barala

Director & Founder

Director's Message
"Barala Hospital is providing advanced multi-speciality healthcare services in this area at affordable cost for every segment of society. My mission is to offer cost-effective and quality services that meet high standards to all patients — whether from Chomu village or a distant town."

Our hospital was built on a simple belief: that the people of rural Rajasthan deserve the same quality of care available in the finest hospitals of any major Indian city. Every investment we make in technology, training, and infrastructure is in service of that belief. We are proud to have served hundreds of thousands of patients and to continue earning the trust of our community.

Our Journey

Key Milestones

1

Foundation of Medical Practice

The Barala family of physicians — with combined experience of 20+ years — establishes clinical practice in Chomu, making specialist care locally accessible.

2

Formal Incorporation (2014)

Barala Hospital and Research Centre formally incorporated — a not-for-profit institution dedicated to the health of the community.

3

Super-Speciality Expansion

Addition of Cardiac Cath Lab, Neuro-Surgery, Oncology, and Urology/Nephrology departments — elevating the hospital to true super-speciality status.

4

Government Empanelments & Recognition

Ranked among leading hospitals in Rajasthan under BSBY; empanelled with ECHS; recognised by ICICI Lombard as a quality healthcare institution.

5

Today — Continued Growth

200+ bedded hospital with 30+ speciality centres, 400+ staff, 50+ TPA tie-ups, and a 4.4-star Justdial rating — continuing to serve the communities of the Jaipur–Sikar corridor.
